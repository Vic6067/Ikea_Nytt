From Nothing to Everything

The DIY furniture that changed homemaking forever.

The purpose of this site is to act as a design study on the progression of IKEA as a brand.

About IKEANytt

This site was created to explore the evolution of IKEA catalogues from the 1950s to the 2000s. In this project I wanted to curate a visual archive of all of my personal favourite pages from every decade. I also wanted to analyze the design trends and cultural influences that shaped IKEA's aesthetic over time. I hope this site serves as a fun and informative history lesson on the iconic furniture company and its impact on home design!
